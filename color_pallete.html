03 — Identity

Signature mark

The dotted inner ring from the Sheilz Coffee seal becomes a recurring UI motif — stamped onto avatars, category icons, and the brand mark itself.

One ring, used everywhere

Every circular element in the product — the cashier's avatar, category icons, the nav brand mark — carries the same dashed ring as the logo's inner seal, so the system always reads as unmistakably Sheilz.
